About Morning Pages

Morning Pages is a writing practice that involves writing three pages of longhand stream-of-consciousness writing every morning.

The goal of Morning Pages is to clear your mind of distractions and to get your creative juices flowing. It can help you overcome writer's block, reduce stress and anxiety, and increase your productivity.

To get started with Morning Pages, all you need is a notebook and a pen. Set aside 20-30 minutes every morning to write three pages of whatever comes to mind, without worrying about grammar, spelling, or punctuation.
